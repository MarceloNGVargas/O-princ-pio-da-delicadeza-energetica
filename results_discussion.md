# Results and Discussion

## δx_min vs Temperature (T)
As temperature increases, thermal noise (k_B T) grows and δx_min worsens.

## δx_min vs Probe Energy (ε)
Increasing energy per probe raises δx_min, worsening resolution.

## δx_min vs Number of Probes (N)
Increasing the number of coherent probes reduces δx_min.

## δx_min vs Coherence Time (τ)
Extending integration time improves resolution.

## 3D Surface δx_min vs T and ε
δx_min is worst when T and ε are large simultaneously; it improves when both approach zero.
